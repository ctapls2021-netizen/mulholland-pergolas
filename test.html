Questions, Answered

Frequently asked questions

Can I start with just a fence now and add a matching gate later?

Absolutely. Because we custom-manufacture everything in our local factory and keep your property's specifications on file, we can easily fabricate and install a perfectly matching gate or additional fencing in the future.

Do you manufacture and install everything in-house?

Yes. Unlike competitors who sub-contract installation or buy pre-fabricated panels, Mulholland designs, manufactures, and installs everything. This ensures complete quality control, a cohesive and unified look, and a single point of accountability.

How does aluminum compare to wood or steel?

Our powder-coated 6063 aircraft-grade aluminum is infinitely superior for coastal and sunny climates. It will never rust like steel or wrought iron, and will never rot, warp, or require staining like wood. It's completely maintenance-free.

Can you automate my gate?

Yes. We specialize in smart automation. We can integrate whisper-quiet motors, remote keypads, and smartphone apps (like Ring or Nest) directly into your custom gate design.
